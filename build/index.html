About Us

WxC International GmbH was founded in 2024 as a diversified company integrating international trade, logistics warehousing, and consulting services for overseas enterprises. The company is headquartered in Schönefeld, near Berlin Brandenburg Airport, known for its convenient transportation. Upholding the concept of connecting Germany with the world and a professional, focused attitude, our team of experts provides the best service to every client.
