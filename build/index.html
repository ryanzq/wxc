About Us

To provide permium service to every customer

WxC International GmbH was established in 2024. It is a diversi- fied company that combines international trade, logistics and warehousing, and also offers consulting and customized services for businesses. The company's headquarters is located in the conveniently accessible Schönefeld, close to Berlin Brandenburg Airport.

The company's mission is to be based in Germany while connecting with the world. We are committed to providing the highest quality service to every customer with a focused approach, and a professional, efficient, and innovative management team.
